The Panorama Hills Water Company (PHWC) operates and manages the water distribution system licensed by the Idaho Department of Water Resources (IDWR) under Water Right 25-7100 that supplies the homes in the Panorama Hills subdivisions (“Alpha” and “Beta” Areas of Panorama Hills and the Altura Verde subdivision, all located on the foothills above Iona, Idaho.) This water right has a “priority date” of 12/16/1977 and covers groundwater used for domestic and irrigation purposes by the property owners (the PHWC “shareholders”) within these subdivisions.
